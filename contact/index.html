For more information about the Partners Affecting Learning (PAL) Evaluation Tool or to schedule a professional development session on your campus to help your team unleash the power of school and business partnerships, please e-mail Dr. Guy Alba at guydalba@gmail.com.

You may also submit your school and business partnership(s) for national recognition by completing the PAL Evaluation Tool® and submitting each of them by November 30th each year. You will be notified if your partnership meets the criteria to be honored as a PAL - Partnership of Distinction® for that year. Special commemorative materials are available only to those partnerships that meet the criteria.
